Fact Sheet

Official residential campus enrollment counts, with various breakouts (gender, ethnicity, high school statistics, SAT/ACT scores, GPA, age, major, etc.) Show More

Timeline

From 1987 to 1991, residential campus enrollment fact sheets were generated for the fall term only. Since Fall 1992, enrollment for the residential campuses has been generated each term. EC enrollment data is typically generated after the completion of a full academic year.

How to Obtain Reports

  • Get it online by clicking on "Published Results" in the menu.
  • Jack R. Hunt Library in Daytona Beach, Reference section (Highlights - LB 2822.75.E424).
  • ERAU Prescott Library, Special Collections.
  • All reports available in IR office.
