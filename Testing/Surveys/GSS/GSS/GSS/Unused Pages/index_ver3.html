How To Apply

Step 1) Complete the online application

Whether you plan on attending one of our more than 150 locations throughout the world, or prefer online instruction, Embry-Riddle Worldwide candidates must complete an online application to begin the process.

Step 2) Send Transcripts

Request transcripts from the registrar's office at all colleges or universities you have previously attended. Transcripts must be sent directly from the issuing institution to be deemed official.

Have documents sent to the University at:
Attn: Worldwide Imaging
Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University
600 S. Clyde Morris Blvd.
Daytona Beach, FL 32114

Special Considerations:

  • International Applicants: Documents from schools outside the U.S. must be sent to an Embry-Riddle approved Foreign Credential Evaluation Agency for translation.
  • Military Applicants: The American Council of Education’s website can direct service members and veterans in ordering official military transcripts.

Step 3) Apply for Financial Aid

  • U.S. Citizens interested in applying for financial aid should complete a FAFSA application.
  • All students can refer to our Financial Aid page for information on funding opportunities through Embry-Riddle and to answer question you may have.

Step 4) Check your E-mail

Remember to check your e-mail regularly for messages from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University. Add @erau.edu as a trusted sender and check your spam filter to insure our correspondence reaches you.
