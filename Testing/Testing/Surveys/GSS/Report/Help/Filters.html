Filters

Data Filters: Located at the top of the page. Filters are used to narrow overall survey data down to specific data points to create reports that are meaningful to individual users. Once filters are chosen they remain in effect until ‘clear filter’ button is applied. When exporting/bookmarking/saving data tables/graphs/chart, data filters remain intact and will appear at the bottom of the table/graph/chart.

Campus: Select Campus to create reports based on overall response by campus (Daytona, Prescott, WorldWide). Default includes all campuses.

       Year: AY (Academic Year) survey data was collected. Choose one or more years

       College: Select College to create reports based on overall response within a College

       Major: Select Major to create reports based on overall response within a Major

       Gender, Ethnicity, International Status, Veteran Status: additional filters used to customize reports.

Current Filter Bar: lists filters that are currently applied to survey data
