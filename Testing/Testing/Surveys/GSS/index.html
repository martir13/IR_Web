GSS

Graduating Student Survey

Administered to graduating students from both residential campuses in order to gather the following outcomes data: placement rates, salary figures, the pursuance of higher education, and the rating of an ERAU education. An in-house survey instrument is utilized.
The administration begins approximately one month prior to graduation and continues for two weeks after graduation.
