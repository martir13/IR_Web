ERAU DATA (Fact Book)

The Fact Book has been developed to provide an easy-to-use, electronic source of information about the University. Although not exhaustive, the Fact Book includes longitudinal information which is often requested of the Office of Institutional Research. Primary emphasis has been given to student-related information. Whenever possible, several years of information and/or graphical depictions have been provided to facilitate comparisons over time. The menus on the left will take you to the individual pages developed for that category.
