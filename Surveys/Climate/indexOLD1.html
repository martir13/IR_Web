CLIMATE SURVEY

Description

Gathers opinions from faculty and staff regarding the ERAU environment. An in-house survey instrument is utilized

Timeline

Faculty and Staff opinion surveys were conducted in Spring 1991 as part of the 1990-92 Self-Study reaffirmation process. Other versions (i.e. Climate Survey) were administered again in the fall terms of 1992 and 1993. Administrations were renewed again in Spring 2002, and follow an annual administration.

How to Obtain Reports

  • Get it online, from the "Published Results" section.
  • Jack R. Hunt Library in Daytona Beach, Reference Section (Climate Surveys - LB 2822.75.E427; Faculty Opinion Surveys - LB 2822.75.E422; Staff Opinion Surveys - LB 2822.75.E423).
  • ERAU Prescott Library, Special Collections.
  • All reports available in IR office.
