The University continually seeks to improve upon the quality of the education provided to our students. Realizing that the delivery of such excellence is through our faculty and staff, we have created a particular objective within the Vision 2010 plan focusing on our workplace environment. Your role within the institution provides an integral part of our overall efforts to achieve success as an organization.

A climate survey has been prepared for the purpose of seeking feedback from you on a number of key indicators. your responses will allow us to better understand our working environment and gain measures of faculty and staff satisfaction. Assessing the health of the organization is among the many measures we need to collect as we follow our strategic plan.

We have developed this climate survey as a web-based instrument. This allows you the opportunity to respond to the questions in both an easy and confidential way. Your information will go directly to our Institutional Research office where all responses will be aggregated and compiled, and a summary report developed. Individual responses to the survey cannot be identified and confidentiality of your responses is assured.

Perhaps most important to this survey activity is what one does with the information. I assure you that we are very interested to hear what you have to tell us. Once we have your feedback, we will determine what actions might be required or processes improved. The credibility of this project rests on our ability to let you know we heard you and are responding in some fashion.

So I encourage you to participate in this climate survey and give us the feedback we seek. Your involvement in this process can only serve to strengthen our organization. Thank you very much.

Dr. Barry Benedict
