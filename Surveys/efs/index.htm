EMPLOYER FEEDBACK SURVEY

Description

Administered to employers of recent graduates. Gathers supervisors' opinions on the graduates' overall performance, the usefulness of general and degree-specific skills, and the graduates' level of competence at those skills compared to graduates from other institutions. The list of employers for the survey is generated from responses to the Alumni Survey. An in-house survey instrument is utilized.

Timeline

The administration method has varied with regard to the length of time since graduation. Currently, employers of alumni are surveyed 1 1/2 years after the alumnus graduated from the Daytona Beach Campus, Prescott Campus, or Worldwide(including Distance Learning). Prior administrations of the Employer Feedback Survey ranged from two to five years after graduation.

How to Obtain Reports

  • Get it online, from the "Published Results" section.
  • Jack R. Hunt Library in Daytona Beach, Reference section (Highlights - LB 2822.75.E435; Reports - LB 2822.75.E4252).
