Worldwide Student Satisfaction Survey

Description

Administered to students currently enrolled at the various domestic and international centers of Worldwide. Questions include: reasons for attending ERAU, overall experience with the university and centers attended, and perceptions on academic programs, instruction, and student services. An in-house survey instrument is utilized.



Timeline

Student opinion surveys were conducted in Spring 1991 as part of the 1990-92 Self-Study reaffirmation process. Beginning in 1995, administrations have been conducted every 2-3 years.
