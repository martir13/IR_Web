Description

The Institutional Research Office is responsible for the design and administration of university-wide surveys pertaining to students, faculty, staff, alumni, and employers, and the analysis of the data collected. Reports related to these surveys can be accessed through the links to the left.

Additionally, Institutional Research conducts other department-level and ad hoc surveys as needed.

For questions related to IR surveys please contact Michael Chronister
