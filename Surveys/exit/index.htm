EXIT INTERVIEW QUESTIONNAIRE
(Daytona Beach & Prescott)

Description

Administered to students who officially withdraw from all classes. Gathers reasons for withdrawing and upcoming plans. An in-house survey instrument is utilized.

Timeline

Administrations have been ongoing since 1988.

How to Obtain Reports

  • Get it online, from the "Published Results" section.
