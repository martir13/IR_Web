Alumni Survey

The Alumni Survey was created to gather information from recent ERAU graduates. The instrument includes questions on current employment, additional education, general and program specific skills. Alumni participation allows ERAU to establish placement rates, track the pursuance of higher education, respond to accreditation requirements, and assess the curricula.
