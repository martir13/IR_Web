CIRP Freshman Survey

Gathers demographic, experiential, and attitudinal information about ERAU's first-time freshmen. Participants also respond to issues such as degree aspirations, major and career plans, and expectations of college. The survey instrument utilized from 1989-1991 was a national one from the Cooperative Institutional Research Program (CIRP), while an in-house instrument was used during 1993-1999. The Fall 2001 administration marked a return to usage of the CIRP survey instrument.
