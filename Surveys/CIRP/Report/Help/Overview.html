Welcome!

Welcome to the help menu. This menu is designed to provide help and resources to assist you in using the reporting tool. You can navigate by using the links in the sections below, or by clicking through the tree on the left side of the window.


Overview:

This reporting tool allows you to click and choose survey years, campus, major, and other demographics to customize reports to fit your data needs. Customized reports can be downloaded in various formats including PDF, image, or as a link to embed within external applications such as Taskstream.


Tour:

A step by step tour is available to introduce you to the various sections and tools available on the website. To access the tour, simply click on the "Getting Started" button on the top of the website menus.


Navigation:

Several levels of tabs allow for navigation between various areas and categories of the survey to get you what you're looking for. For more help on navigation, see Navigation.


Charts:

Survey data charts are fully interactive and can be customized to suit your data reporting needs. For a detailed look on how to use these interactive charts, see Charts.


Filters:

Often times data is useful when broken out into various demographics. Filters allow for data to be displayed based on these categories. For more information on how filters work, see Filters.
